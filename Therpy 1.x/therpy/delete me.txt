Properties (Public, defined via decorators):
    od      --  optical density
    app     --  atoms per pixel
    Ii,If   --  background corrected Ii and If counts
    si,sf   --  background corrected si and sf
    Ii_raw  --  rotated and cropped, non background subtracted Ii, If
    rawdata --  rawdata from file, unbinned, unrotated
    props   --  dict of various properties used for calculations

Methods (Public):
    infoplot  -- plot crop and bg_width boundries and final od
    plot      -- plot final od
    set_prop  -- intelligently set properties
    __print__ -- print all relevant information

Imaging Properties (optional):
    image info: name, path
    preparation:
        cropi, cropset:
        subsample: 
        rotate: rotation angle in degrees
        order: list containing ['rotate','crop','subsample']
    background: 
        bg_width, (not yet implemented) bg_images
        bad_light: fractions of light with wrong polarization
    physics parameters:
        Isat: saturation counts per camera pixel per us
        time: imaging time in us
        pixel: size of camera pixel onto atoms in um
        method: one of the following {'BL','sBL','dBL'}
        sigma, sigmaf: provide sigma in SI or sigmaf * sigma_0_Li
